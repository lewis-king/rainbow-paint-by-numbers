# Privacy Policy for Rainbow Paint by Numbers

**Last updated:** January 2, 2026

**Rainbow Paint by Numbers** ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("you" or "your"), especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

### 1. No Data Collection
We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal information from our users. 
* We do not require you to create an account.
* We do not track your location.
* We do not collect usage analytics or behavioral data.
* We do not use third-party advertising networks.

### 2. Local Storage
All game progress (such as painted pictures and unlocked levels) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never uploaded to our servers.

### 3. Children’s Privacy
Our app is designed for children and families. We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
